ALGO2.0 is an experimental electronic music experience whose sound is defined by its users digital anonymous data. The aim of ALGO2.0 is to explore new sounds through the blurring of boundaries between the listener and the music, seeking to create a personalized sound and music experience for its users. By participating in ALGO2.0, its users have the opportunity to be part of a unique sound composition through a generative and unrepeatable audiovisual experience. The first sound installation of ALGO was developed under the context of an artistic residence called Sound Exchange, promoted by Artspace Letschebach, an art gallery in Karlsruhe, Germany.
